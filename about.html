Assessment framework

How we grade Quran recitation

Every recitation assessment scores five categories on a 0–5 scale.

Category What we listen for
FluencySmooth, confident flow without stops or restarts.
MakharijCorrect articulation points for each letter (huroof).
TajweedRules of Idgham, Ikhfa, Madd, Ghunnah, Qalqalah etc. applied correctly.
WaqfStopping rules and signs respected — recitation flows naturally.
AccuracyNo letters missed, added, or substituted.

A teacher's notes accompany every score. Weaknesses become next week's focus.
